Why ACTIVEBUYS?

Whether through featured Active Deals or our Active Resources, ACTIVEBUYS gets your business in front of individuals and employers who understand the importance of getting active and staying healthy and are passionate about supporting the businesses that help them do so. Align your brand with active, healthy living and help be part of the difference!

  • We Create

    a community of like-minded individuals who embrace an active, healthy lifestyle.

  • You Deliver

    the best possible products & services to help them fuel their active, healthy lifestyle.

  • They Support

    your business and create healthier, happier communities to live and work.

 

ACTIVEBUYS is NOT a daily deal site. We are an exclusive community built for a targeted demographic of individuals who live, or are seeking to live, an active, healthy lifestyle. The goal of ACTIVEBUYS is to bring together Nashville’s best businesses with our lifestyle seeking members, creating a win-win for all.

ACTIVEBUYS puts you in control. We help you design a customized marketing package that is right for your business. Contact us today to get started.
